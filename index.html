About Me

I am a Scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. My research interests lie in the application of optimization and AI techniques to create decision support systems for use case applications, with a particular focus on manufacturing and maritime sectors. My current work includes harbour craft scheduling and maritime predictive maintenance.
